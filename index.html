As the owner and lead technician of Roy's Furniture Repair, I bring over twenty years of dedicated experience to every project. My journey in furniture restoration began in 2000, when I had the privilege of working alongside seasoned professionals who taught me the fundamentals of the craft.

What started as learning the basics evolved into a passion for self-improvement and mastering advanced techniques. I've specialized in wood repair, troubleshooting, and problem-solving for both power and manual recliners, while also providing upholstery services on a case-by-case basis.

After earning recognition as a 5-star technician with several established companies, I made the decision to establish my own independent practice. This allows me to provide personalized service and maintain the high standards that have earned the trust of countless satisfied customers throughout Northern Virginia.

My Mission

"To provide excellent furniture repair service that surpasses clients' expectations consistently. My immense job satisfaction as a furniture repair technician is only matched by the fulfillment I get from my clients' complete satisfaction."
